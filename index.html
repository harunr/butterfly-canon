BUTTERFLY CONSERVATION

Monarch Butterflies have a unique lifestyle – American Monarchs go through three regenerations – and then migrate all through the US, via Texas and into Northern Mexico where they overwinter in trees for around 5 months.

Sadly, more and more of their habitat in Mexico is being destroyed by intensive and often illegal farming. We donate to the Monarch Butterfly Conservation Fund – which aims to conserve the magic of the monarch migration.
